Decision Problem

Decision-makers need to understand whether earlier MASH treatment can reduce future transplant demand, relieve waitlist pressure, and improve system-level outcomes.

Strategic Insight

Earlier treatment may create value beyond direct drug effects by changing downstream demand in a shared, capacity-constrained transplant system.

About

Why Industrial Engineering matters for healthcare data and evidence strategy

My Industrial Engineering background gives me a systems-level approach to healthcare decision-making. I was trained to design decision frameworks that connect data, operations, uncertainty, constraints, and business goals.

That perspective is valuable for data strategy, HEOR, HTA, and RWE because healthcare organizations do not just need more data. They need a roadmap for how data is collected, managed, analyzed, and translated into secure, actionable evidence.
